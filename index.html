About

The person behind the pixels

I'm Shehab Mohsen, a frontend developer with over six years of experience shipping interfaces for startups and scale-ups. I care about the 60fps details: gesture-driven transitions, meaningful microinteractions, and component systems that scale without slowing down.

I work primarily in the React and Vue ecosystems, build design systems with Tailwind and Shadcn, and manage state with Zustand and Redux. TypeScript is my default — I don't ship JavaScript without it.

What sets me apart: I bridge design and engineering. I prototype in code, not Figma. I benchmark animation performance. I treat CSS as a systems language, not a styling tool.
